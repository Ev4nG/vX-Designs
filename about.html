About Us

Our Story

Founded in 1892, vX originally made door knobs! Our founder, Vincezo Xtasi, realised the world was changing. Hes saw an ever increasing social change and knew he must be the one the spearhead this change.

In 1901 Vincenzo opened his first fashion studio in his home town in Italy.He released the first ever vX Collection. The rest, as they say, is history.
